Schedule

Day 01: HA NOI - MOC CHAU (250 km:L/ D)

06h30: Bus and Tour guide pick you up at your hotel in Ha Noi Old Quarter and then departure to visit Moc Chau by bus about 4- 5 hours.

09h30: Bus stop on the windy Thung Khe pass to take pictures and admire the majestic mountains of Hoa Binh.

12h00: Arrive in Moc Chau town, enjoy lunch at the restaurant. And then come to homestay checkin.

In Afteroon: Tour guide will take you to visit:

  • Ang Village pine forest –  Coming here, visitors can enjoy the peacefull and quiet atmosphere, mingle with the nature and human culture here. And this is also an ideal place for you to take a little walk.
  • Nui Moc tourist area is located at an altitude of more than 1000m above sea level. Located at Moc Chau Farm, Moc mountain tourist area is one of most attractive tourist attractions in Son La province with charming mountain beauty.
  • Moc Chau fruit seasons: Depending on the time, the guide will take you to take pictures with the typical flower gardens and orchards of Moc Chau. Surely you will be surprised and impressed by these vibrant and attractive beauties (Note: how these gardens and gardens are completely depends on the weather and people’s planting plans).
  • Pure white plum blossom season from late December to early February.
  • Fresh peach blossom season on the occasion of the Lunar New Year every year in January to February.
  • Vast season of white cabbage on the hills before and after the Lunar New Year in February.
  • Plum season ripens in April to June.
  • The buckwheat flower season is bustling from about September to December.
  • Strawberry picking season in winter months and some different times.
  • And a variety of local specialties spread throughout the year.

19h00: Enjoy your dinner and overnight at homestay in Moc Chau town.

 

Day 02 | MOC CHAU - BACH LONG GLASS BRIDGE (B /L /D)

07h00: Enjoy break at homestay.

08h00: Tour guide pick you up to visit “Bach Long Glass Bridge”:

  • Bach Long Glass Bridge was buil in Lun village, Muong Sang commune, Moc Chau district, Son La province, located in the complex of resorts and entertainment Moc Chau Island. This is a new tourist destination in Moc Chau that is attacting the attention and attention of a large number of domestic and foreign tourists.
  • Moc Chau tourist destination – Bach Long Glass Bridge was built on the available natural terrain of the Northwest mountains with a total length of to 632m, spanning two 290m-long mountains and 342m-long cliffs, 2.4m wide with 2.4m x 3m extra-large glass panels, the height of the bridge deck to the ground is 150m. Apply 9D effect technology with 60 simulated visual and sound effects to create a feeling. Appraised by Kuraray – Japan according to international standards. The entire bridge system is used super tempered glass Saint gobain of France, including 3 layers, 40mm thick, which can withstand 450 people at the same time.

12h00: Enjoy the lunch at the local restaurant. Then the trip continues.

13h30: Arrive Dai Yem waterfall – A waterfall, although small, has a charming beauty. Legend has it that this waterfall is the girl’s bib to save the boy from the flood. (Here you can buy tickets to visit Love Glass bridge – the first glass bridge in Vietnam. Or you can choose walk around)

16h00: we are come back to homestay relax.

19h00: Enjoy the dinner at the restaurant, then you are free to explore Moc Chau town, market, shopping. Stay overnight a more night in homestay.

DAYS 03 | MOC CHAU – HA NOI (B/L/)

07h00: You enjoy breakfast at the homestay. After you check out room.

08h00: Bus and tour guide pick you up to the trip continues:

  • Tea Hill in the Heart, perhaps this is not only the most beautiful tea hill of Moc Chau Plateau, but also the most beautiful tea hill of Vietnam because of the round and winding green tea beds as far as the eye can see, romantic and peaceful feeling. This is also the raw material area to produce the famous brand, which is O Long tea.
  • Na Ka Plum valley with wild and novel landscape. Especially in the season of pure white plum blossoms or in the autumn when the fruit is ripe, it seems that Na Ka puts on her beautiful and wonderful clothes (Note: Na Ka is only entered during the plum blossom season or when picking flowers, fruit, when it is not the flower or fruit season, the guide will spend a longer time on other attractions).

10h30: Get on the bus back to Ha Noi.

13h00: Half way the bus to stop in the restaurant. Enjoy your lunch.

18h00: Arrive in Old Quarter Ha Noi. Finish the trip.
