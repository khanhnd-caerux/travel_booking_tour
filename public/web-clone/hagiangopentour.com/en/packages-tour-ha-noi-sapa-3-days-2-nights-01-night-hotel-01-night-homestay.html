Introduce

Packages Tour Ha Noi - Sapa 3 Days 2 Nights. The city of Sapa in Northeastern Vietnam is a delightful old frontier town high in the mountainous region of the same name. It has, in recent years, become the center of trekking holidays in the country. Many of Vietnam’s ethnic hill tribes live here including the Hmong, Dao, Giáy, Pho Lu, and Tay.

The population of the area is estimated to be around 32,000 of which only 5% are Viet (or Kinh). The area is famous for its terraced hillsides on which the locals cultivate rice. These terraces present a real picture postcard beauty.

Schedule

Day 01 | HA NOI – SAPA – CAT CAT VILLAGE – SLEEP IN HOTEL (350km:L/ D)

06h30: Bus or Limousine Bus pick you up at the point then depart to SaPa, start 5 hours driving to SaPa.

12h30: Arrive in SaPa, checkin hotel and have lunch.

14h00: Local tour guide pick you up and start a 2 km trekking to Cat Cat Village of Black Hmong trible. On the way you have a chance to attend SaPa market to see 3 ethnic minority groups selling or exchanging their products with each other such as, vegies, weaving brocads, handicrafts, embroideries or traditional clothes… And then walk down the villages to see a beautiful waterfall and power plant built by the French during colonial time in 1925. From here you will have 40 minutes to enjoy performances shown by boys and girls from the village. And then trek 3,5 km up to SaPa town.

16h30: Enjoy picnic lunch by the side of the river or have lunch with a local family on the way to Sin Chai Village Then next 3 km to Sin Chai, the village located at the foot of Fansipan Mountain, “the roof of Vietnam and Indochina”. And then back to hotel in SaPa town.

19h00: Enjoy your dinner and overnight at hotel in SaPa town.

 

DAYS 2 | SAPA – FANSIPAN – TA VAN VILLAGE (B /L /D) – SLEEP IN HOMESTAY

07h00: Enjoy break at hotel.

08h00: Tour guide pick you up to visit Fansipan. Here you can choose to buy tickets to take the Fansipan cable car (price: 850.000 vnd – pay by yourself) about 3 – 4 hours. And then walk back to the hotel.

12h00: Enjoy the lunch at the hotel or restaurant, then you are checkout hotel treckking to Ta Van Village.

13h00: Trekking through the rice terraces and along Muong Hoa Stream to the black Hmong village of Lao Chai to see the spectacular landscape of Hoang Lien Son Mountain. The trekking distance is about 12 km and it lasts for 3- 4 hours.

16h30: Arrive Ta Van Village – checkin homestay of the Dzay ethnic group people. Spend time enjoying the mountain life on Sunset. You may take part in cooking local dishes with the family and enjoy dinner in peace.

19h00: Enjoy your dinner and overnight at homestay in Ta Van village. In the evening, you can join the local bar nearby with music and socialize with other tourists.

DAYS 03 | TA VAN – GIANG TA CHAI - SAPA – HA NOI (B/L/)

07h30: You enjoy breakfast at the homestay.

08h30: Keep walking to the rice paddies where you have a nice photo session of the amazing surrounding landscapes, a trek though the bamboo forest, a stop to have panoramic view of Giang Ta Chai village – home of the red Dao people. The trekking distance is about 3 km and it lasts for 3 hours.

11h00: Trek through the Giang Ta Chai Bridge to the main road, the bus pick you up come back to SaPa town.

12h30: Enjoy your lunch in hotel, after lunch we will check out room and waiting the bus pick up back to Ha Noi.

13h00: You will be transferred to the bus. You are will return Hanoi by bus.

20h00: Arrive bus office in Old Quarter Ha Noi.
