Introduce

Packages Tour Ha Noi - Sapa 2 Days 1 Night. The city of Sapa in Northeastern Vietnam is a delightful old frontier town high in the mountainous region of the same name. It has, in recent years, become the center of trekking holidays in the country. Many of Vietnam’s ethnic hill tribes live here including the Hmong, Dao, Giáy, Pho Lu, and Tay.

The population of the area is estimated to be around 32,000 of which only 5% are Viet (or Kinh). The area is famous for its terraced hillsides on which the locals cultivate rice. These terraces present a real picture postcard beauty.

Schedule

Day 01 | Ha Noi – Sapa – Sin Chai – Cat Cat Village (350km:L/ D)

06h30: Bus or Limousine Bus pick you up at the point then depart to SaPa, start 5 hours driving to SaPa.

12h30: Arrive in SaPa, checkin hotel and have lunch.

14h00: Local tour guide pick you up and start a 2 km trekking to Cat Cat Village of Black Hmong trible. On the way you have a chance to attend SaPa market to see 3 ethnic minority groups selling or exchanging their products with each other such as, vegies, weaving brocads, handicrafts, embroideries or traditional clothes… And then walk down the villages to see a beautiful waterfall and power plant built by the French during colonial time in 1925. From here you will have 40 minutes to enjoy performances shown by boys and girls from the village. And then trek 3,5 km up to SaPa town.

16h30: Enjoy picnic lunch by the side of the river or have lunch with a local family on the way to Sin Chai Village Then next 3 km to Sin Chai, the village located at the foot of Fansipan Mountain, “the roof of Vietnam and Indochina”. And then back to hotel in SaPa town.

  • OPTION 01: SLEEP IN HOTEL IN SAPA TOWN.
  • OPTION 02: SLEEP IN HOMESTAY IN TA VAN VILLAGE.

19h00: Enjoy your dinner and overnight at hotel in SaPa town.

 

DAYS 2 | Sapa – Fansipan – Ha Noi (B /L /)

07h00: Enjoy break at hotel.

08h00: Tour guide pick you up to visit Fansipan. Here you can choose to buy tickets to take the Fansipan cable car (price: 850.000 vnd – pay by yourself) about 3 – 4 hours. And then walk back to the hotel.

12h00: Enjoy the lunch at the hotel or restaurant, then you are checkout hotel waiting the bus pick you up back to Ha Noi.

13h00: You will be transferred to the bus. You are will return Hanoi by bus.

20h00: Arrive bus office in Old Quarter Ha Noi.
