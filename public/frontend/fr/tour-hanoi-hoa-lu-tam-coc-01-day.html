Introduce

Tour Hanoi - Hoa Lu - Tam Coc  Hoa Lu & Tam Coc are located in Ninh Binh province, about 110 km from the south of Hanoi. 

Hoa Lu was the ancient capital of Vietnam under Dinh & Le Dynasty. It was witnessed many important historical events and was the formation of Hanoi nowadays.

Tam Coc is a zone of Vietnam that elapses between rice paddies, bathed by the Ngo Dong River punctuated by karstic rock formations and limestone caves. It means three small caves and known as Halong Bay on land.

 

Schedule

ITINERARY

8:00- 8:30: Our tour guide and driver will pick you up from your hotel around Hanoi Old Quarter. After staying on the bus about an hour, you take a short rest around 10- 15 minutes in the half way.

10:30- 11:00: Arrive at Hoa Lu, visit ancient capital, Dinh & Le Temples then the bus takes you to the local restaurant in Tam Coc for having lunch. 

13:30- 14:00: Go to Tam Coc harbor, get on the small boat for seeing the peaceful scenery and three small caves in about two hours.

15:30- 16:00: Have time for riding bicycle in 40 minutes. (Optional)

16:30- 17:00: Leave Tam Coc and come back to Hanoi by bus.

19:00- 19:30: Arrive at Hanoi Old Quarter. Tour ends.
