Introduce

 

Ha Giang Tour: 3 Nights, 2 Days - Easy Driver (Motorbike Tour). Ha Giang, the prominent region of Vietnam known for its cool climate and fresh air, is an alluring tourist destination. Visiting Ha Giang offers the opportunity to witness numerous majestic landscapes, including the Dong Van Plateau, the twin mountains of Quan Ba, the Meo King Palace, the Ma Pi Leng Pass, the Lung Cu Flagpole, as well as several captivating waterfalls and intriguing caves. These sights promise a wonderful and unforgettable experience.

 

 

 

 

 

Schedule

Day 01: Ha Noi – Ha Giang City (350km: L/D)

06h30: Limousine bus will pick you up at your hotel in Ha Noi Old Quarter.

(Note: Our bus can pick you up at Noi Bai Airport or at another location in city, please tell us in advance)

11h00: The bus stops at Tuyen Quang city for a break.

13h00: The bus arrives in Ha Giang City. Enjoy your lunch at our restaurant. Afterward, our bus will drop you off at the hotel for check-in room and some rest.

15h00: The tour guide will pick you up at the hotel to explore the city, visiting: 

  • Km0 square, HaGiang Museum, Nui Cam Mountain view.
  • Thanh Thuy - Chinese border.
  • Thon Tha, Ha Thanh Village. This is a small, beautiful village about 6 km from Ha Giang City, inhabited by the Tay people. You will find ancient houses on stilts, terraced rice fields,and a stunning waterfall – perfect for a leisurely walk.
  • 19h00: Enjoy dinner and return to the hotel. Then, you’ll have some free time.

Day 02: Ha Giang City – Lung Cu – Dong Van Town (175 km: B /L /D)

07h00: Have breakfast at the hotel and proceed to check out of your room.

07h30: The car and tour guide will pick you up. Immediately after, embark on a sightseeing trip to Ha Giang Loop:

  • Cross to Bac Sum Pass – the first slope welcoming you to the “Dong Van Karst Plateau GeoPark’’. At this point, we’ll simply drive though.
  • Heaven’s Gate Quan Ba – Stop to capture the picturesque view of the Nui Doi twin mountains at the top of the slope.
  • Can Ty Pass – Reaching an altitude of 1000- 1200 meters above sea level, marvel at the breathtaking and majestic scenery.

12h00: Enjoy a local food lunch in the small town of Yen Minh before continuing the trip.

  • Tham Ma pass – Stop at the summit of the slope to enjoy the stunning landscape. To reach this point, you’ll traverse 9 interesting passages.
  • Visit Sung La valley and Lung Cam – Explore this small village featuring classic Hmong houses, including a visit to Pao’s house.
  • Hmong king’s Palace – This historically significant site used to be the residence of the most powerful Hmong people and was considered their palace in a distinct Chinese architectural style.

17h00: Arrive in Dong Van town and check into the hotel.

  • Dong Van district marks the starting point of Vietnam, sharing a border stretching hunderds of kilometers with China. The population mainly comprises various ethnic minorities, includingHmong, Tay, Nung, Lo Lo, Co Lao, San Chi, San Diu,... among others, with Hmong with being the predominant group (about 70%). One of the most renowned attractions is the Dong Van old Quarter, historically a significant trading center and maintaining its importance to this day. On Sunday mornings, Dong Van Old Town bustles with a lively market where locals from all around gather to buy and sell goods.

19h30: Ẹnjoy dinner at the Old Quarter, followed by a leisurely walk.

Day 03: Dong Van – Lung Cu - Meo Vac. (60 km: B /L /D)

07h00: Enjoy breakfast. Afterward, check out of the hotel and proceed to Lung Cu.

(On Sunday mornings, you have the opportunities to visit the local market of ethnic people in Dong Van town)

  • Lung Cu flag pole – Here, you’ll find the beautiful Lo Lo Chai village nestled at the foot of Long Son Mountain, which is home to the Lo Lo minority people. The village maintains itsunique houses and is inhabited by friendly locals, making it a great place to visit on foot.

11h30: Return to Dong Van town for lunch. Afterward, continue driving on the road famously known as “Happiness”.

13h30: Ma Pi Leng Pass – Situated at an altitude of over 1200 meters above sea level, offering a spectacular mountainous view between Vietnam and China, with the Nho Que river below. A great spot for sightseeing and photography. You can also stop for tea or coffee at Panorama Coffee (self-payment of about 60.000 VND)

 

14h30: We explore Nho Que river by boat and then visit Meo Vac town.

16h30: Arrive at Pa Vi village in Meo Vac and check into the homestay.

19h00: Enjoy dinner at homestay and unwind.

Day 04: Meo Vac – Ha Giang City – Ha Noi  (60 km: B /L /D)

07h00: Enjoy breakfast and check out of the homestay before returning to Ha Giang city. En route, you have the opportunities to visit:

  • Meo Vac town – Meo Vac is a highland district adjacent to the China border. The district has a sparse population and is predominantly inhabited by the Hmong people, accounting for about 70% of the population. The Hmong people have a custom of living in the high and challenging mountain ranges. 

 (on Sunday mornings, they gather to trade and meet at Meo Vac town market)

  • Lung Tam village – A handicraft village of the Hmong people located in the middle of Lung Tam valley.
  • Nam Dam village – A beautiful and peaceful small village of the Dao people, approximately 2 km from Quan Ba town, renowned for its houses built with local soil and herbal medicines.

13h00: Enjoy lunch at Quan Ba town.

15h30: Arrive at the Ha Giang city office and await the bus for the return journey to Ha Noi:

+ OPTION 1: 16h00 - Limousine bus - 22h00. Arrive in Ha Noi.

+ OPTION 2: 21h00 - Sleeping bus – 04h00. Arrive in Ha Noi.

The trip concludes. Thank you and we look forward to seeing you again!
